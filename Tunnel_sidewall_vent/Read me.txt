The sidewall extraction tunnel fire database aims to facilitate the application of AI algorithms in smart tunnel firefighting, and it is affiliated to the following paper.

Y. Jiang, T. Zhang, S. Liu, Q. He, L. Li, X. Huang. (2021) Full-scale fire tests in the underwater tunnel with sidewall smoke extraction (submitted to TUST)

All the data are stored in 8 folders following the test numbers.

In each folder, the temporal vent temperature profile, and temporal temperature data (T1-T9) are presented.

This work is funded by a themed fund "SureFire: Smart Urban Resilience and Firefighting" in Hong Kong. For more information, please visit our website: https://www.bse.polyu.edu.hk/PolyUFire/Project.html.
